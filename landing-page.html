Summer Quarter Financial Aid

Summer quarter begins on Monday June 30, 2014.  Federal and state financial aid programs cannot be disbursed or bookstore accounts activated for summer quarter prior to Tuesday July 1, 2014. The Financial Aid Office sends a confirmation email to students via the campus assigned email account when disbursements have been sent to Higher One for disbursement. Due to a high volume of disbursements at the beginning of the quarter, please allow extra processing time.
